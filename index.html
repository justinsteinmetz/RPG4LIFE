Identity is not a fixed trait.
It is a build — assembled from roles assigned by others,
choices made under constraint,
and scripts repeated until they feel like personality.

You will complete a character sheet.
Then the dungeon will run you through it.

Assigned — inherited, not chosen
Chosen — selected by you
